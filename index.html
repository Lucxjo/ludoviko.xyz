Who am I?

Well, for a start I should probably say that my name isn't Ludoviko. I use that name on the internet, no I'm not going to tell you my real name just call me Ludo or Ludoviko. Ludoviko is actually a translation of my name into Esperanto, the shortened down form should be Lucxjo but I prefer Ludo. I am from the Midlands in the United Kingdom and work as an Electronics Engineer.

In my freetime I work on various programming projects, including apps, games and Minecraft mods. A lot of these will never be released but this website is to showcase those that I do release. I open source all of my projects that aren't connected to my job even if I have no intention to release them. I figure that someone will find them useful, right?
